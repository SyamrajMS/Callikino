⚡ **The Key to Lightning Speed: Stream Copying vs. Re-Encoding**

🛠️ **When Does FFmpeg Take High Processing Power?** 
FFmpeg will only start heavily consuming your CPU/GPU when you apply visual changes that alter the underlying pixels—like adding text overlays, kinetic zooms, or color grading LUTs.
